About my work

The pieces of work you see here is work I completed in my second year of college. I completed them in a module called Visual Design Fundamentals. For forst piece is a double page spread I made in Adobe Indesign as I was tasked with creating a magazine. The topic could be whatever I wanted but the theme was constructivism.

The topic I done my magazine on was famous skyscrapers and buildings. The second piece of work you can see was a book cover I made for a potential book on the video game Red Dead Redemption. This cover was made with Adobe Illustrator and a clip art which I drew over. I also got the letters and resized them so they would look like a revolver gun.
